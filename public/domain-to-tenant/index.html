Domain to Microsoft Entra Tenant Lookup

Look up the Microsoft Entra tenant behind any verified domain name. sub2tenant queries Microsoft Graph tenantRelationships to return the tenantId, displayName and defaultDomainName for the tenant associated with the domain.

This helps with verifying an organization’s Microsoft 365 tenant, troubleshooting sign-in issues or confirming tenant ownership during onboarding and incident response. No sign-in is required, and domains or results are not logged or stored.

Email address input

Email addresses are accepted as a convenience. When an email such as user@contoso.com is provided, sub2tenant extracts the apex domain (contoso.com) and performs the same domain-to-tenant lookup. Email values and derived domains are not persisted.
